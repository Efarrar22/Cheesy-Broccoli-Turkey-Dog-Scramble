Cheesy Broccoli Turkey Dog Scramble

A savory and protein-packed breakfast option that brings together turkey hotdogs, cheddar cheese, and broccoli for a quick and satisfying meal.

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 15 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 turkey hotdogs, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup broccoli, chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter for cooking

Instructions

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced turkey hotdogs and cook for 2-3 minutes until slightly browned.
  3. Add the chopped broccoli and saut√© for another 2-3 minutes until tender.
  4. Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble, stirring occasionally.
  5. Stir in shredded cheddar cheese until melted and evenly distributed.
  6. Add hot sauce, salt, and pepper to taste, then serve immediately.
